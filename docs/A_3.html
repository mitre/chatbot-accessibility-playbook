Appendix 3:
User Questionnaire Items

These questionnaire items assess the chatbot’s accessibility for users. Incorporate these questions into user questionnaires throughout the design process to evaluate the accessibility of the chatbot. For questions that are subjective, use a 1 to 5 or 1 to 7 Likert scale. For questions that are objective, compare the user’s response to an ideal value.
